## Project Overview

**constructon** is Zimbabwe's first modern villa construction company website, designed to attract and convert customers seeking Western-style smart villas. The platform targets diaspora homebuyers and local professionals with transparent pricing, smart home technology, and quality construction.

### Value Proposition

> "Western-quality smart villas, built in Zimbabwe with complete transparency"

### Target Personas

| Persona | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Diaspora Returner | 40s professionals abroad seeking trust & transparency |
| Local High-Achiever | Successful professionals wanting modern design & smart tech |
| Young Power Couple | First-time builders seeking affordable stylish starters |
| Investor | Buyers seeking turnkey solutions & ROI |

## BMAD Methodology Phases

### Phase 1: Brainstorm
Customer persona development, cross-industry insights, and customer journey mapping using Role Playing, Cross-Pollination, and Six Thinking Hats techniques.

### Phase 2: Model (PRD)
41 functional requirements and 32 non-functional requirements covering villa discovery, pricing tools, lead capture, trust building, content resources, SEO, and analytics.

### Phase 3: Architect
Technology stack decisions (Next.js 16, Tailwind CSS, Sanity CMS, Vercel), component architecture, and implementation patterns.

### Phase 4: Design (UX)
Visual design system, component specifications, user journey flows, and responsive/accessibility strategy.

### Phase 5: Develop (Epics)
7 epics with 45 implementation stories covering the complete development roadmap.

## Epic Overview

1. **Site Foundation & Homepage** - Mobile-friendly foundation with navigation
2. **Villa Discovery & Showcase** - Browse plans, photos, floor plans, filtering
3. **Pricing & Financial Tools** - Transparent pricing, financing calculator
4. **Lead Capture & Communication** - WhatsApp, forms, phone contact
5. **Trust & Credibility** - Team profiles, testimonials, company story
6. **Content & Resources** - FAQ, investment info, smart home showcase
7. **Analytics & Optimization** - Tracking and conversion measurement
